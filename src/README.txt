QuadEdit

Capabilities required:

   - Read a patch from the Quadraverb
   - Write a patch to the Quadraverb
   - Load a patch from a library of patches (on the PC disk)
   - Save a patch to a library of patches (on the PC disk)
   - Read a bank of patches from the Quadraverb
   - Write a bank of patches to the Quadraverb
   - Load a bank of patches from a library of patches (on the PC disk)
   - Save a bank of patches to a library of patches (on the PC disk)

   - Swap two patches
   - Copy and paste data from patches (i.e. delay, reverb etc settings)
   - Support more than 100 patches in a library
   - Compare two patches
